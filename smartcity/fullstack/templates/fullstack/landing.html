    About our service

    SmartCities is a place to search for services in a location to avoid spending time and money for services that are not worth it.
    If you ever found yourself struggling to find out the best of a service in a place you are a stranger, and at the end have to just go by trial and error? probably you SmartCities would have been handy if it had been in available. This is what we relieve you of. Just select your country, region and city and enter the service you are interested in to get the list of all related services.

    Want to provide a service?

    Becoming a service provider is easy. All you have to do is to sign and create service provider profile for the service you wish to render. Each service has a seperate profile. An existing user can as well provide a service without having to create a seperate account. Just create profile for your service and it will be linked to your account.

    What problem do we solve?

    Our service address these key issues:

    • Accessiblity
    • Service quality
    • Service provider and customer relationship
    • Regulation of service
